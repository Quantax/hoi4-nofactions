Starting factions no more!

This mod removes all starting factions on game start. After that factions can be created normally via diplomacy, focuses or events.

Compatible with other mods, even total conversions, such as Millenium Dawn and Kaiserreich!

If you are looking for completely faction-free game, check out my other mod [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=749498075]No Factions[/url].
